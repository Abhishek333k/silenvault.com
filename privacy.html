Privacy Policy for SilenVault

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SilenVault ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you utilize the silenvault.com network, including our utility applications (tools.silenvault.com), digital interactive media (games.silenvault.com), and e-commerce platform (store.silenvault.com).

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to operate our software, process transactions, and serve relevant advertising.

  • Account & Transaction Data: When you purchase techwear or software licenses, we collect your name, email, and shipping address. Payment details are processed securely by our third-party gateways (e.g., Lemon Squeezy, Shopify) and are not stored on our servers.
  • Automated Log Data: Our servers automatically record your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the timestamps of your interactions with our tools and games.
  • Local Storage: Certain applications on tools.silenvault.com utilize your browser's LocalStorage to save your progress or configurations offline. This data remains on your device.

2. Third-Party Advertising & Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements across our network. To comply with global privacy standards and Google’s Publisher Policies, we disclose the following:

  • Advertising Cookies: Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.
  • Personalized Advertising: Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the internet.
  • User Opt-Out: You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with:

  • Service providers essential to our operations (hosting, payment processors, analytics).
  • Law enforcement, if legally compelled to do so to protect our rights or the safety of our users.

We employ commercially reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
